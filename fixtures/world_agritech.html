        What are the Benefits? 

        Pitch, exhibit, and connect with industry leaders through the summit's exclusive start-up opportunities.

        Network from your own branded Start-Up Arena booth, among 60+ innovators in a space dedicated to spotlighting early-stage innovation. We provide wi-fi, power, and two delegate passes making it easy to network, join workshops, or participate in roundtables. 

        If you’re a start-up ready to increase your visibility and power up your network, or an accelerator with an exciting portfolio of agtech companies, we want to know more! 

        Get in Touch (opens in a new tab)

         

        Themed pitching sessions give start-up founders the opportunity to pitch their solutions to the summit's influential audience. There's a prize for the audience's favourite pitch - apply today to get involved! 

        Promotion begins as soon as you sign up, with a website profile, social media promotion, amplification of news and a pre-summit press release shared to our global audience. 

        Get in Touch (opens in a new tab)

         

        Are you bringing a cohort of companies? Our dedicated program for accelerators and regional innovation hubs allows you to participate as an Innovation Partner. Showcase a selection of companies in the Start-Up Arena and secure valuable stage time to highlight their innovations.

        Discuss Opportunities (opens in a new tab)

         

        We know that it’s the personal connections you make at a summit which deliver the most value to you and your business.

        Host your own virtual booth on the summit platform, and gain early access to plan your networking outreach. The platform remains open for one month post-event, giving you plenty of time to book 1-1 meetings and follow up on those all-important connections..

        Connect with international retailers, investors, start-ups and technology leaders over drinks and food at the various networking breaks and the happy hour, in the Exhibition & Networking area.
