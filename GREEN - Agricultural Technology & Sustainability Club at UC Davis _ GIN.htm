    Welcome to GREENat UC Davis

    The Green Innovation Network (GIN) at UC Davis is a student-led AgTech organization passionate about tackling environmental and agricultural technology challenges through collaboration, hands-on projects, and cutting-edge research. Join UC Davis's premier green tech club to connect, create, and drive change toward a greener future.

    Our Mission

    To foster a community of students passionate about agricultural technology (AgTech), environmental sustainability, and green innovation at UC Davis.

    What We Do

    We organize AgTech workshops, speaker series, and projects focused on sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, and environmental conservation. As UC Davis's leading green tech club, we bridge the gap between innovation and sustainability.

    Our goal is to empower students with the knowledge and skills to become leaders in the agricultural technology sector and green economy.

    Our Vision

    To create a greener future by promoting sustainable practices and innovative green technology solutions at UC Davis and beyond.

    We envision a world where environmental sustainability and agricultural innovation are at the forefront of every industry and community.

    Make an Impact

    Your contribution directly supports GREEN's initiatives in sustainable innovation.

    Help us provide resources, workshops, and events for students passionate about environmental solutions.

    Enable GREEN to expand its reach and impact within the UC Davis community and beyond.

    Join Us

    Become a part of a passionate community dedicated to making a difference.

    Join GREEN (GIN) to unlock exclusive AgTech resources, seize new opportunities in agricultural technology, and connect with UC Davis's premier green tech network that powers your personal and professional growth.

    Collaborative Community

    Engage in an inclusive network that values diverse ideas and perspectives.

    Learn, share, and grow at hands-on sessions that inspire creativity.

    Real-World Projects

    Get involved in initiatives that bring sustainable solutions to life on campus and beyond.
